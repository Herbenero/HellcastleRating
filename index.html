Hellcastle Rating
(HR)

HR is a unified rating system for board games, card games, dice games, darts, ladderball, and similar match-based play. It accounts for wins, losses, ties, points scored, margin of victory, and the number of opponents in a match. Use the webapp below to log matches by date, enter multiple players, and automatically calculate each player’s evolving HR.

HR Formula

HR = 1000 + Results + Points + Strength + Volume Results reward wins, soften losses, and recognize ties. Points scored adds steady credit for offensive production. Strength adjusts for beating higher-rated players. Volume gives a light reward for sustained participation.

Result Scoring

Win = +24
Tie = +10
Loss = -8 Each match also applies a multiplayer adjustment, point differential bonus, and opponent-strength modifier based on current HR.
